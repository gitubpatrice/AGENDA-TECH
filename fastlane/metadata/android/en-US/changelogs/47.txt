v0.5.0 — Snooze, restore, backup reminder

• Reminder notifications now carry a
  "Snooze 10 min" button — without touching
  the next reminders of a recurring event.
• An empty calendar offers to restore a
  backup: the state of a fresh install on a
  new phone.
• The app now reminds you to back up — but
  rarely: never before you have something to
  lose, never if nothing changed since the
  last one, and "Later" buys two weeks.
